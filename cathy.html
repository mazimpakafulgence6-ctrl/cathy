About Cathy Agribusiness

📍 Based in Mbarara City, Western Uganda — Cathy Agribusiness has been a trusted name for high-quality agricultural inputs and professional farming advisory since 2015. We bridge the gap between smallholder farmers, commercial growers, and premium agrochemical manufacturers.

We supply certified herbicides, fungicides, foliar feeds, and biological solutions to combat pests and improve soil fertility. Our team of agronomists offers end-to-end project advisory: from land preparation, crop selection, to post-harvest handling.
